About Me

I’m an HR and Operations professional from Chittagong, Bangladesh, with a strong bias toward action and execution. Recently completed my BBA in Human Resource Management from BGC Trust University Bangladesh. I’ve always believed that real learning happens beyond the classroom.

That mindset has shaped everything I’ve done.

From managing HR operations in a global remote environment to facilitating UNDP programs impacting over 2,000+ students, I’ve consistently stepped into roles that demand ownership, structure, and results. I don’t just support systems, I build and improve them.

At the same time, I took a completely different path outside the corporate space.

I founded তাঁবু লাগবে (Taabu Lagbe?), a tourism venture focused on eco-friendly camping experiences across Bangladesh. What started as an idea turned into real campsites, real customers, and real operations. From handling guest experience to managing logistics and growth, I built it from the ground up and turned it into a functioning business.

Here’s what defines me: I move between structure and creativity with ease. I can manage people, build systems, and execute ideas in the real world and I’m driven by one thing, creating impact that people can actually see and feel.
